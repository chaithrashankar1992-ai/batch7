Our Story

Craft. Fire.
Passion.

Ember & Ash was born from a singular obsession: what happens when exceptional ingredients meet the ancient art of live fire. Our chefs travel seasonally to source the finest cuts, the freshest produce, and the most compelling wines.

Every plate is a conversation between technique and instinct — guided by fire, finished with intention.
